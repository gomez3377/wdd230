Citizen's Voice Concerns About Franklin City Park

FRANKLIN, ID — Citizen’s of Franklin City voiced their concerns about the Franklin City Park during a city council meeting on September 11. Vandalism, loud music at night, personal injury, and open doors to city shops after hours were some of the issues brought up.

Aaron Bergman, Franklin City Attorney, clarified liability issues for the city from injury on park property and possible solutions for the future. Council member Deidre Burgess offered to donate a high definition wireless camera for surveillance purposes, while John Packer suggested using game cameras. The council also discussed placing signs on the property to list the parks hours of operation.
