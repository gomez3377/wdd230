Target Audience

Who is the Target Audience?

The target audience is for those who are traveling and need to know the local weather conditions and events going on for the day.

Personas

Persona 1: Out of Town Travelers

This demographic are people who don't know much of the area display on the website. They either seek this information to plan ahead of what to expect when traveling one of the towns listed in the site to see how the weather is and whether there are local events that are happening in that town.

Persona 2: Local Residents

These are the locals who need to see the weather conidtions of the area. They can see whether there is going to be poor weather that will affect them so that they can plan or attend local events. They need to easily see and read the weather condidtions and access all the information going on in their local area.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

A family wants to visit some relatives in Preston, Idaho for Christmas for a couple of weeks. They are from out of the state so they don't know much of the area around Preston. They will need to know up to date information on the weather and road conditions of Preston. They will also want to check out any events that are happening around Preston during the holidays.

Scenario 2

A group college kids want to go to Soda Springs to check out the geysers there. The weather is there for them to see but they want to easily access the information about the geysers and to check out some events that are going on in the area.

Scenario 3

A parent or a child living in Preston, Idaho who is checking the not only the local weather of the town but any news about school closures.

Scenario 4

A family who is planning to travel out Fish Haven, Idaho, wants to know what kind of activities are going to be there in Fish Haven, whether there are hotels or or the kind areas they can camp, or if it's even worth camping depending on the weather.

Scenario 5

Drivers who are driving across Idaho want easy access to up to date accurate weather conditions especially during the winter where they need to know any snow storms will cause delays or road closures.
